Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Kova ("we", "us") is a workout-tracking app. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and the choices you have. Questions: hello@kovafit.ca.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address and a username/display name, used to create and secure your account.
  • Workout data — exercises, sets, weights, reps, body-weight entries, streaks, and personal records that you log.
  • Profile photo (optional) — if you set a profile picture or attach a photo to a shareable workout card, the image is stored on our behalf.
  • Social activity — the people you follow, and the cheers and comments you post on workouts.
  • Sign-in identifiers — if you sign in with Apple, Google, or GitHub, we receive a unique identifier and basic profile info (such as name and email) from that provider to create your account. If you use Sign in with Apple and choose to hide your email, we receive a private relay address.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, or advertising identifiers, and we do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

How we use your information

  • To provide the app: store and display your workouts, stats, and history.
  • To power social features: show your activity to people you allow, and theirs to you.
  • To send local reminders/notifications you opt into (these are scheduled on your device).
  • To keep the service secure and functioning.

We do not sell your personal information.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored using Supabase (Postgres database, authentication, and file storage), which acts as our data processor. Access to other users' data is restricted by row-level security so people only see what you've shared with them.

Notifications

Kova schedules reminder notifications locally on your device. You can turn these off at any time in the app's Settings or in your device's system settings.

Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account from inside the app: Settings → Delete Account. This erases your profile, workouts, stats, follows, comments, and uploaded photos. The deletion is immediate and cannot be undone. You may also email us at the address above to request deletion.

Children

Kova is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
