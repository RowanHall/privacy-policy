RPE and RIR, explained

RPE (rate of perceived exertion) rates how hard a set felt, from about 6 to 10. RIR (reps in reserve) is the flip side — how many reps you had left. They're linked simply:

RIR = 10 − RPE

RPE is a judgement call and gets more accurate with experience. Estimates here use a standard RPE-to-percentage chart and are most reliable in the 1–8 rep range. Related: one-rep max calculator.
