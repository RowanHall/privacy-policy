How to read your score

DOTS and Wilks both scale your total by your bodyweight, so a 60 kg and a 100 kg lifter can be compared on a level field. For a full squat + bench + deadlift total, a rough guide:

DOTSLevel
Under 200Beginner
200–300Novice
300–400Intermediate
400–500Advanced
500+Elite

These bands assume a full powerlifting total. If you enter a single lift the score will be much lower — that's expected. Standards vary by federation; treat this as a guide, not an official ranking.
