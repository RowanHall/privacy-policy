Frequently asked

How do I delete my account?

In the app, go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently erases your profile, workouts, stats, follows, comments, and uploaded photos. The deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

If you can't sign in, email hello@kovafit.ca from the address on your account and we'll delete it for you.

I can't sign in with Apple / Google / GitHub.

Most sign-in issues clear up after a force-quit and reopen. If it still fails:

  • Check your device's date & time are set automatically.
  • If you used Sign in with Apple and chose "Hide my email", make sure that relay address is still active in your Apple ID settings.
  • Email us with the provider you used and the time of the attempt.
I logged a workout but it's missing.

Pull to refresh on the History tab — if it doesn't reappear, the session likely failed to sync before you closed the app. Send us the date and exercise name and we can check the server-side logs.

How do streaks and personal records work?

A streak counts consecutive days with at least one logged set. Personal records are tracked per exercise — heaviest weight at each rep count, and estimated one-rep-max. New PRs are flagged the moment you log a set that beats your previous best.

How do I turn off notifications?

All reminders are scheduled locally on your device. Turn them off in the app's Settings → Notifications, or from your device's system Settings under Notifications → Kova.

Can I export my workout data?

Not yet from inside the app — but if you email us we'll send you a CSV of your full history. Built-in export is on the roadmap.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email hello@kovafit.ca with steps to reproduce (for bugs) or the use case (for features). We read every message.
